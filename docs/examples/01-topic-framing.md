# Example 01: Topic Framing

## Goal
Turn a broad topic into a testable hypothesis and acceptance criteria.

## Suggested command flow
1. `/start "Open-source AI assistant infrastructure"`
2. `/research-idea "Open-source AI assistant infrastructure"`
3. `/citation-audit "research/papers/draft.md"`

## Expected outputs
- `research/ideas/idea-brief.md`
- `research/hypotheses/hypothesis-v1.md`
- A short novelty statement with target audience and metrics.

## PASS criteria
- Hypothesis is testable.
- Scope is explicit.
- Novelty claim is connected to at least one evidence source.
