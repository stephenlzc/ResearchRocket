# Path-Specific Rules

- `research-docs.md` for `research/**` and `production/**`
- `experiment-code.md` for `research/experiments/**` and `tools/**`
- `paper-docs.md` for `research/papers/**` and `deliverables/**`
- `data-lineage.md` for `research/data/**`, `research/datasets/**`, `research/experiments/**`, and `artifacts/**`
- `data-quality.md` for `research/data/**`, `research/datasets/**`, and `research/results/**`
- `analysis-integrity.md` for `research/results/**`, `research/experiments/**`, and `deliverables/**`
- `reproducibility.md` for `research/experiments/**`, `artifacts/**`, and `production/**`
- `figure-standards.md` for `research/results/**`, `research/papers/**`, and `deliverables/**`
- `review-gate.md` for `research/papers/**`, `deliverables/**`, and `production/**`
- `citation-integrity.md` for `research/literature/**`, `research/papers/**`, and `deliverables/**`
- `submission-docs.md` for `research/papers/**` and `deliverables/**`
- `readme-i18n.md` for `README.md` and `README.*.md`

Gate behavior:
- Integrity and reproducibility blockers can fail the final gate.
- Style sanity remains warn-only and does not block by itself.
