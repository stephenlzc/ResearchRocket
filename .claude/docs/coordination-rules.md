# Coordination Rules

1. PI owns final research decisions.
2. Literature analyst owns source quality and novelty map.
3. Data engineer owns dataset lineage, cleaning, and schema consistency.
4. Experiment engineer owns executable pipelines and run logs.
5. Quant analyst owns metric validity, statistics, and uncertainty reporting.
6. Figure lead owns figure traceability and visual consistency.
7. Writing lead owns paper coherence and section completeness.
8. Reproducibility auditor owns independent rerun evidence.
9. Submission manager owns venue-format packaging checks.
10. Peer reviewer owns final critique and gate recommendation.
11. Cross-role conflicts escalate to PI.
